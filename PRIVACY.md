# QuietBar Privacy

QuietBar operates locally on your Mac.

## Accessibility permission

QuietBar uses macOS Accessibility permission to inspect menu bar item names and positions so it can display and manage those items. This permission can be disabled at any time in System Settings.

## Data handling

- Menu bar information is processed locally.
- QuietBar does not require an account.
- QuietBar does not upload menu bar information to a server.
- QuietBar does not include analytics or advertising tracking.

## Revoking access

Open **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility**, then disable or remove QuietBar.
