# QuietBar Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

QuietBar is designed to operate locally on your Mac. QuietBar does not collect,
transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Information Processed on Your Mac

QuietBar may process menu bar geometry and, when you grant Accessibility
permission, available menu bar item names and application locations. This is
used only to organize the menu bar and display matching application names and
icons inside QuietBar.

This information remains on your Mac and is not sent to a server.

## Data Collection

QuietBar does not include:

- accounts or sign-in;
- analytics or telemetry;
- advertising or tracking SDKs;
- cloud storage or synchronization;
- remote databases or APIs.

The App Store privacy declaration is **Data Not Collected**.

## Local Preferences

Language, live refresh, launch-at-login, automatic compact delay, shortcut, and
onboarding choices are stored locally through macOS preferences and Apple
system services.

## Accessibility Permission

Accessibility permission is optional for the core reveal and compact action.
When enabled, QuietBar uses it locally to obtain available menu bar item names
and match application icons.

You can revoke access at any time in:

**System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility**
